The Waanterbach-crag is a beautiful sandstone cliff, hidden in the forest next to the small town of Berdorf. Often overcrowded during weekends, Belgian climbers Wanne Libot and Seb Berthe used the much quieter weekdays and spring conditions to make quick work of two classic lines: Cima Ovest and Hasta la vista gringo.
